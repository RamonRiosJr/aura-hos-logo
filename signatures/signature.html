Email Signatures

Professional, highly-compatible HTML email signatures

💡 IMPORTANT COMPATIBILITY NOTE: Email clients (like Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail) heavily restrict custom CSS, Javascript, and animations to prevent security risks. For this reason, we cannot inject the fully animated React or CSS-based "Aura Animated Head" directly into the signature structure. Instead, the signature uses a safe, static vector/image fallback structure hosted remotely or embedded securely to ensure perfect rendering across all devices, capturing the professional tech aesthetic.
